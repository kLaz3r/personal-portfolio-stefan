# Salam de Bacău - Product Label Design & Mockups

## Project Details

**Client:** Salam de Bacău
**Type:** Product Label Design & Mockups
**Finish:** Professional product packaging design

## Description

Product label design and mockup collection for Salam de Bacău (Bacău Salami) brand. The project features comprehensive label designs for traditional Romanian salami products, showcasing brand identity, product information, and quality certifications. The mockups demonstrate how the label designs appear on actual product packaging, providing realistic preview of final product presentation.

## Tools Used

- Adobe Illustrator (Vector design & layout)
- Adobe Photoshop (Mockup creation & image editing)
